# Test Prompts for ExcelSpeak

## Basic Analysis:
- "Show me the first 10 rows"
- "Count how many rows I have"
- "What columns are in my data?"

## Statistical Queries:
- "Calculate the average salary"
- "Find the maximum value in each column"
- "Show me summary statistics"

## Grouping & Aggregation:
- "Group by department and show average salary"
- "Count how many employees in each department"
- "Show department-wise salary distribution"

## Filtering:
- "Show employees with salary above 70000"
- "Find records from 2022"
- "Filter by engineering department"

## Advanced:
- "What are the trends in my data?"
- "Find correlations between columns"
- "Predict future trends based on current data"
